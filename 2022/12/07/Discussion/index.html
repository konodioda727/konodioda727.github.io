Unit 1

B: Hey, Kaiqing, we have learned English for almost six years.What’s the most difficult thing about English learning for you? And Do you have any methods to learn it well?

A: The most difficult thing about English for me is probably grammar. There are so many rules and exceptions to remember, and it can be frustrating trying to figure out the correct verb tense or word order.

A: Emmm, for the methods,one that has helped me improve my grammar is constantly reading and writing in English. The more exposure I have to the language, the more I am able to internalize the grammatical rules and apply them in my own writing.

Another method that has been helpful is finding resources that explain grammar rules in a clear and concise manner. This can be in the form of grammar books or websites with interactive exercises. Practicing with these resources has allowed me to better understand and retain the rules of English grammar.

Overall, learning English is a continuous process and requires patience and dedication. It’s important to find what works best for you and to be consistent in your studies.

A:What about you, what troubles you the most?

B: I find it challenging to improve my pronunciation and accent.

A: Have you got any ideas to get over it?

B:To help overcome these difficulties, I have found a few good methods for learning English well. Firstly, I try to immerse myself in the language as much as possible. This means listening to English music, watching English movies and TV shows, and speaking English as much as I can with native speakers or other learners.

Another helpful method is to practice regularly. This can include things like doing exercises in a textbook, participating in English language exchange programs, or even just chatting with friends in English.

Overall, the key to learning English well is persistence and a willingness to make mistakes. By regularly practicing and immersing myself in the language, I am confident that I can continue to improve my English skills.

Unit 4

A:Hey, have you heard of that new Internet celebrity, Elon-Musk? I can’t believe how much influence he has on social media

B:Yeah, he’s definitely one of the most well-known Internet celebrities out there. And he’s not the only one. There are so many people who have become famous through social media, like Cai-XvKun and Ding-Zhen

A:Yeah, it’s crazy how the Internet can give people such a large platform to share their thoughts and ideas. But do you think it’s a good thing that these Internet celebrities have so much influence?

B: It’s definitely a mixed bag. On the one hand, it’s great that internet stars are using their platform to raise awareness for important issues and to inspire their followers to take action.promoting positivity and healthy behaviors. There are so many internet stars who are using their platform to inspire people to be kinder, more accepting, and to take care of their mental health.Like musk,he has garnered attention for his ambitious plans and innovative ideas, such as his proposal for a high-speed transportation system called the Hyperloop.That pushes the society forward. But on the other hand, Internet stars with a large following may spread misinformation or promote conspiracy theories, which can have negative consequences for society, such as causing mistrust in institutions or leading to dangerous behaviors.

A: Yeah, I can see what you mean. And some of these Internet celebrities can have a negative influence on their followers too, especially if they’re promoting unhealthy or irresponsible behavior like DingZhen who was found smoking regularly.

B:Definitely. It’s important for people to be mindful of the influence they have on others and to use their platform responsibly. It’s also important for Internet celebrities to be honest and transparent with their followers, and not to present a false or misleading image of themselves.

A:Can’t agree more. I think it’s important for internet stars to consider the potential impact of their content and to be responsible with the platform they have. They have a large following and can influence people’s behaviors and decisions, so it’s important for them to be mindful of that.

B:Yeah, I agree. It’s definitely a double-edged sword. the influence of internet stars on society can be both positive and negative, and it’s important to critically evaluate the content and messages shared by internet stars and to consider the potential impact on society.

Ubit 5

A:Hi, I wanted to talk to you about some of the inconvenient aspects of our school.

B:Of course, I’m always open to hearing feedback from students. What are some of the issues you’ve noticed?

A:Well, one thing I’ve noticed is that the bathrooms are often dirty and not well-maintained. It’s really unpleasant to use them, and it’s not hygienic either. It’s a basic necessity for students to have clean and functional bathrooms, and it’s frustrating that this isn’t being addressed.

B:Thank you for bringing that to my attention. I’ll make sure to address the issue with our facilities team and ensure that the bathrooms are being cleaned and maintained regularly. We want all of our students to feel comfortable and have access to clean facilities while they’re at school.

A:Another thing is that the lunch line is always so long and it takes forever to get food. It’s frustrating because I don’t have much time to eat during my lunch break, and by the time I get my food, I barely have any time left to eat. It’s also frustrating because there aren’t many options for students with dietary restrictions, and it’s difficult to find something that fits their needs.

B:I understand your concerns. We’ll work on improving the efficiency of the lunch line and also expanding the variety of options available for students with dietary restrictions. We want to make sure that all students have enough time to eat and that they have access to nutritious and appealing food options.

A:That would be great. Another issue is the lack of parking spaces on campus. It’s really difficult to find a spot, and I often end up being late to class because of it. It’s especially frustrating when I have an early morning class and I can’t find a spot close to my building.

B: I’ll make sure to bring this up with our administration and see if we can find a solution, such as adding more parking spaces or exploring alternative transportation options for students. We want to make sure that getting to and from school is as convenient as possible for all of our students.

A:Thanks for listening to my concerns. It’s really appreciated.

B: No problem. I’m glad you came to me with these issues. It’s important for us to address them and make improvements to create a better experience for all of our students. Please don’t hesitate to bring any other concerns you have to my attention. We value your feedback and want to make sure that our school is a welcoming and supportive environment for all of our students.

Unit 6

B: Hey, Kaiqing, one question ,if there is low-paid job you like,would you like to give it a shot? Or, if there is a well-paid job but you don’t like it, would you choose it?

A: It’s a really tough decision because on the one hand, the higher-paid job would provide more financial security and stability. It would be easier to pay bills and save for the future, and it might even allow for more leisure and travel opportunities. But on the other hand, I think it’s really important to enjoy and be fulfilled by your work. I think for me, I would probably choose the low paid job that I enjoy, as long as it doesn’t put me in financial strain. I value the satisfaction and enjoyment I would get from the job more than the extra income from a higher paying job. What about you?

B: I think I would choose the higher paying job, even if it’s a bit boring. I value financial stability and the ability to support myself and my family. While I would love to have a job that I enjoy, I think the extra income from a higher paying job would be worth it for me.

A: Yeah, I can see that. It’s definitely a personal decision and everyone has different priorities. Speaking of work, what kind of boss would you prefer to work for? One who is inefficient but kind, or one who is efficient but very strict with employees?

B: Personally, I would rather work for a boss who is inefficient but kind. I think it’s important to have a good relationship with your boss and to feel supported and valued in your work. Even if things aren’t running as smoothly as they could be, I would rather have a positive work environment where I feel comfortable and supported. I think it’s important to have a boss who is open to feedback and suggestions and who values their employees.

A: Well, I’m the opposite.I think I would prefer to work for a boss who is efficient but strict. I value efficiency and productivity more highly and feel that a strict boss is necessary to achieve these goals.I also feel that a strict boss holds me to high standards and helps me to perform at my best. As for work, do you prefer working hard or working smart?

B: I think it’s important to find a balance between working hard and working smart. Working hard alone won’t necessarily get the job done efficiently, and working smart without putting in effort isn’t effective either. It’s important to find a balance and put in the effort and dedication needed to get the job done well. I think both are important and finding the right balance is key to success.

A: Definitely. I agree completely. It’s important to find that balance and put in effort and dedication to achieve success. I think that’s especially true in today’s competitive job market. It’s not just about putting in the hours, but also being strategic and working efficiently to get the job done.

B: I think that’s the key to a successful and productive work!Thanks for the great conversation
