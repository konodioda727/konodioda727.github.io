Unit 1

B: Hey, Kaiqing, we have learned English for almost six years.What’s the most difficult thing about English learning for you?

A: Well, the spelling errors really made me headache, I just couldn’t remember it’s order.

B: I also used to suffer a lot from it! But I then started to go over to words on radio,and download an app called “BaiCiZhan”,those really worked! You can give it a shot.

A: Bravo! Then what about you? What troubles you the most?

B: I’m poor in grammar.Have you got any idea to handle this?

A: Emmm, If I were you, I would try to search for videos attached to grammar on the Internet and buy some grammar books to learn. Don’t get worried. I believe that it won’t be a trouble for you!

Unit 3

B: What do you like doing in your spare time?

A: I like to play badminton and sometimes go out wandering.Both of which get me relaxed physically and mentally.If have friends around,I will probably choose to cycle alongside the east lake with them. It usually will be fun and will pull me out of the heavy works for instance.

B: Yes, it is of great importance to balance between work and rest.

A: Oh,I almost forget! The weekend is approaching,have any plan?

B: Absolutely have, what’s approaching is not only weekend, but loads of exams as well. So I chose to stay in the library to revise the lessons and get my homework done.I may got tired, so I planned to read some books at that time to relax.

A: Well, also a good choice,but just remember to relax at some times. As the saying goes “All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy”remember not to push yourself hard.Take it easy.

Unit 4

A: Nowadays, Internet celebrities are gaining massive impression in the internet society.Like Li-Ziqi, who shares rural life in the countryside and delicious cuisine, got millions of fans following and cultivated Chinese culture in many people’s mind.She is a positive figure of internet celebrity.

B: Can’t agree more! Another example is professor LuoXiang, who share legal knowledge in a interesting way, also contributes a lot for society and is been widely loved. I like him a lot.He is the few in number that choose this field and work well.

Unit 5

A: Having studied in school for a while, but there still have some inconvenience.

B: Well, feel free to come up.

A: Firstly, the poor heat in library is really annoying, I think I will catch a cold.

B: Don’t get worried, the government is trying their best to solve this problem, and the work is planned to be done in one week.Just hold on, my boy.

A: What’s worse, I recently found that the plugs in our domitory no longer work,every of it! Our computers’ battery can’t stand for a long time. And we can’t learn programming without computers.

B:Oh! Sorry to hear about that.Could you please tell me your dormitory number so that I can assign maintenance workers to check.

A: Of course,sir!121 Thank you very much.

B: My pleasure, hahah.Thank you for your opinion as well!

Unit 6

B: Hey, Kaiqing, one question ,if there is low-paid job but it’s of your type,would you like to give it a shot? Or, if there is a well-paid job but you don’t like it, would you choose it?

A: I think I will choose low-paid jobs they like, one will have more passion while doing things he like though salary is low, he enhances his state of mind.

B: There’s another question popped up in my mind just now. There are two bosses for you to choose, one is inefficient but kind, the other is efficient but strict,which do you prefer?

A: As for me,I would prefer the one who is efficient but strict. It is efficiency that matters in a group.One who don’t manage the whole group well can’t be considered as a great leader.

B: Then what about you yourself,do you like working hard or working smart?According to your previous replies ,I guess you will choose working hard,don’t you?

A: Of course! You are in my shoes! What about you,which do you prefer?

B: the smart one I guess. Sometimes efforts aren’t equal to the returns.As the saying goes”Think twice before you leap”,I would spend more time and effort in finding more efficient ways.It’s efficient and energy saving.
